About

Meet Reed Kruger

At Clutch & Clean Detailing, a clean car isn't just about appearance — it's about peace of mind. Detailing started as a hobby for Reed: the crisp look, the fresh interior smell, the satisfaction of bringing a car back to its best self. That hobby became a passion, and that passion became a mission — helping people feel good about their vehicles no matter how hectic life gets.

We all have those crazy days. Work piles up, errands run long, the kids spill something in the back seat — and the last thing anyone wants to figure out is when they'll find time to clean the car. That's where Clutch & Clean comes in: high-quality, convenient, mobile, and reliable.

Whether it's a full detail or a quick touch-up, we show up ready to make your car feel brand new. Fast, friendly, and always thorough.
